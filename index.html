Work

Here are a few samplings of my work thus far. I am always writing more, so please contact me if you are intersted. I always enjoy sharing and swapping ideas ideas, and nerding out.

SofaSkipping is a site I created, inspired by CouchSurfing. The premise of the site is a hospitality and social networking site, where members can stay with other members (on their sofa!) for free.

FuzzBall is a JavaScript browser game that is playable on both desktops and handheld devices. It is based on the Windows 95 game JezzBall. The goal is to divide the balls apart by drawing walls between them and claiming as much empty area as possible. Just don't let the wall be hit while it is forming. It can be quite the challenging game
